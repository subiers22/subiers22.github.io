What are your rates?
We charge $50 p/h for video editing. Other than that, everything else is set based on your specific project and your needs. Some of the determining factors are:

Do you need us to shoot video?
Are we required to travel for any purpose?
How much content do we need to produce?
I have already shot the video, will you just edit for me?
Yes! If you or someone else has shot a project already, we will be happy to take that footage and turn it into something awesome! Conversely, we're also happy to take video for you to edit yourself, or pass on to someone else to edit.
